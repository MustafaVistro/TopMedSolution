What We Do

Complete Revenue Cycle Solutions

Medical Billing

End-to-end billing services that maximize your collections. We handle claim submission, follow-up, denial management, and patient billing — so you focus on patient care.

Provider Credentialing

Fast, accurate credentialing with all major insurance networks. We manage applications, follow-ups, and re-credentialing to keep your practice in-network and revenue flowing.

Revenue Cycle Management

Full RCM oversight from patient registration to final payment. We identify revenue leaks, reduce days in A/R, and deliver detailed reporting so you always know where your money is.

Virtual Medical Assistant

Dedicated virtual assistants handling prior authorizations, appointment scheduling, insurance verifications, and patient follow-ups — giving your staff time back every single day.

Medical Coding

Certified coders ensuring every service is accurately coded using ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS. We reduce coding errors, prevent claim denials, and optimize reimbursement rates.

Coding & Compliance

Stay audit-ready and fully compliant with HIPAA, OIG, and payer guidelines. We conduct regular audits, staff training, and policy updates to protect your practice from penalties.
